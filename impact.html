Successes

  • Helped reduce impaired driving deaths by nearly 50%
  • Raised national drinking age to 21 in the U.S.
  • Advocated for ignition interlock laws
  • Backed BAC limit reductions (.08% in US, .05% in Canada)

Challenges

  • 10,000+ impaired driving deaths still happen yearly
  • Repeat offenders remain an issue without stricter enforcement
  • Opposition from some lobbyists and industries
